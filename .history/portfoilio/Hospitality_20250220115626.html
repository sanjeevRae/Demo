OverView

AWDDEL has a strong presence in the hospitality and travel sector, offering a blend of personalized services and exceptional guest experiences. The company has an extensive history in hotel management and hospitality, having previously owned and operated the renowned Jomsom Hotel & Resort. Under AWDDEL’s leadership, the hotel provided a luxurious retreat for travelers exploring the beauty of Mustang, Nepal.

In a strategic move to enhance the brand's identity and reach, Jomsom Hotel & Resort was sold and subsequently rebranded as Shinta Mani Mustang, a part of the prestigious Shinta Mani Hotels collection. This transformation highlights AWDDEL’s expertise in hospitality management, emphasizing a commitment to high standards and memorable experiences for all guests.

AWDDEL continues to play an influential role in shaping the travel and tourism landscape, with a focus on innovation, luxury, and cultural integration.
